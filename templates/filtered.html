How To Increase Filtered Leads Rate?

What are Filtered Leads?

Filtered leads are leads that have been verified by the Evaboot filtering algorithm.

Each time you export leads, Evaboot verifies if they match the filters applied during your LinkedIn Sales Navigator search.

The first column of the export file indicates whether the lead matches your search criteria.

The second column provides reasons for any leads marked as NO MATCH*.

Typically, 20% to 30% of leads do not align with the original search filters.

You can choose to download all leads or only those marked as filtered for each export.
